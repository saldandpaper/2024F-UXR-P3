Limitations

The study was conducted in very short notice and period of time, as a result the participant diversity is fairly limited. The questions were also created in a rush. As a result, some question sound very similar.

The places we contacted were heavy YouTube users, but the forum would get lost in massive chat logs. For interviews to get more detailed information, certain subjects needed more time to prepare or be contacted, causing the loss of 2 out of 6 people we contacted.

Future Considerations

If we break down the limitations, we get to 3 issues. Question language and flow, preparation time, distribution channels.

Question Language and flow

The questions should be run and event tested at least 2 times to make sure they have the correct flow and understood correctly

Preparation Time

Due to everything being made on a short notice, question quality and participant diversity is low because there was not enough time to find people to interview or find participants for surveys. The only solution to lack of time is to allocate more time.

Distribution channel

Places distributed the survey. Allowed it to only be distributed in a random chat, so it was drowned in a sea of chats from other users. While that is better than nothing, I suggest finding places that are willing to put it in announcement channels.
